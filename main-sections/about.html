About Me

Hey! I'm Suriya Kumar S, a 2nd-year student pursuing B.Tech in Biomedical Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. I genuinely believe that one of the best things we can do for others is to share knowledge. Apart from academics, I’m also a curious learner, a YouTube content creator, and someone who loves to explore and explain.

"Biomedical Engineering is where I get to apply my passion for science in ways that directly impact people’s lives. It’s the perfect blend of problem-solving and innovation, making it an exciting field to be a part of. I’ve learned that sometimes, the best solutions come from thinking outside the box—and yes, I’ve had my fair share of ‘creative’ attempts at fixing things with whatever tools I had on hand!"

I enjoy taking tough topics and making them simple—for myself and for others. Whether it's classroom concepts or something new I’ve discovered online, I like presenting it in a way that's easy to understand and apply in the real world.

I’m not someone who just memorizes notes. I focus more on understanding deeply and finding how to relate that knowledge to practical situations. To me, learning is not a one-time task—it’s a lifelong process of connecting, questioning, and improving.
