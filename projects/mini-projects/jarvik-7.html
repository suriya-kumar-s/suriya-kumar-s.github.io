Introduction to the Jarvik 7 Total Artificial Heart (TAH)

The Jarvik 7 TAH is a mechanical heart designed to provide life-saving support for individuals with end-stage heart failure. Developed by Dr. Robert Jarvik, it serves as a bridge to heart transplant for patients awaiting suitable donors.

  • Fully pneumatic power source
  • Two blood pumps replacing the heart's ventricles
  • Mechanical valves for blood flow regulation
