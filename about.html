About Me

Hello! I'm Suriya Kumar S, currently pursuing my second year of B.Tech in Biomedical Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. I believe that one of the greatest gifts we can offer to others is knowledge—sharing what we learn, experience, and understand helps everyone grow stronger together.

I’m someone who genuinely enjoys breaking down complex things into simple, understandable forms. Whether it's an academic concept, a tech idea, or a real-life scenario, I love finding clarity and spreading that clarity through creative means. I don’t believe in simply memorizing for the sake of marks—I believe in truly understanding things and applying them where they matter.

Apart from being a Biomedical Engineering student, I’m also a long-time learner and a passionate content creator. As a YouTuber, I explore Tamil devotional traditions and local cultures, sharing meaningful content with my audience. This hobby keeps me grounded and connected to my roots while pushing my creativity forward.

Biomedical Engineering is a powerful fusion of healthcare and technology—and I’m excited to be part of this evolving space. From studying sensors and systems to exploring artificial intelligence in medicine, the journey so far has been both challenging and rewarding. I look forward to contributing meaningfully to the field, while continuing to explore, learn, and share beyond the boundaries of a textbook.
