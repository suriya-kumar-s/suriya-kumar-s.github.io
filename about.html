About Me

I'm Suriya Kumar S, currently in my 2nd year of B.Tech in Biomedical Engineering at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. I believe that one of the greatest gifts we can offer the world is knowledge sharing. I’m not just a student—I’m a long-time learner, a passionate YouTuber, and someone who constantly explores new ways to grow.

Biomedical Engineering fascinates me because it blends biology with technology to bring real impact. Whether it’s about sensors or surgeries, I’m always curious. And yes—just like how Iron Man turned a car battery into an arc reactor, I once tried fixing a loose wire with a pen cap. Spoiler: didn’t work, but now I carry tape!

I enjoy breaking down complex concepts and making them accessible—not just for myself, but for others too. Whether it’s a tough topic in class or something I’ve learned online, I love simplifying things and turning them into easy-to-understand visuals or content.

I'm not someone who just mugs up notes; I focus on understanding and connecting what I learn to the real world. For me, learning is about discovery and application. If a concept doesn’t make sense yet, that just means I haven’t looked at it from the right angle—yet!
