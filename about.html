About Me

I'm Suriya Kumar S, a second-year Biomedical Engineering student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. I believe that the most valuable gift one can offer is the power of shared knowledge.

While I'm passionate about the complexities of biomedical systems, I'm equally driven by the joy of breaking down difficult topics into simple, digestible content—whether it’s in the classroom, a research setting, or even my YouTube channel. I'm not here to mug up. I’m here to understand and apply.

Biomedical Engineering itself is a blend of medicine, mechanics, electronics, and innovation. From sensors that mimic stethoscopes to algorithms diagnosing diseases—this field is all about bridging health and technology.

P.S. I tried explaining biomedical imaging to my dog. He walked away unimpressed—but at least I understood it better.
