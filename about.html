About Me

Hello! I'm Suriya Kumar S, a passionate 2nd-year Biomedical Engineering student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur.

My goal is to simplify complex medical and engineering concepts so they can be understood, applied, and even reimagined for real-life use. I don't believe in just memorizing theories—I dive deep to understand them and find ways to connect them with the real world.

Whether it's decoding ECG waves or understanding how sensors work in prosthetics, I strive to bridge the gap between textbook knowledge and hands-on innovation. Learning for me is not just academic—it's about making an impact.
